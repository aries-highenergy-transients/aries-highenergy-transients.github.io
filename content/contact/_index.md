### How to Reach ARIES

The main campus of ARIES is situated at Manora Peak, which is about 9 km from the Nainital bus stand. 

*   **By Air:** The nearest airport is Pantnagar Airport (PGH), located about 70 km away, with flights from New Delhi.
*   **By Rail:** The nearest railway station is Kathgodam (KGM), located about 35 km away, connecting to major cities like Delhi, Lucknow, and Kolkata. Local taxis are easily available from Kathgodam to Nainital and Manora Peak.
